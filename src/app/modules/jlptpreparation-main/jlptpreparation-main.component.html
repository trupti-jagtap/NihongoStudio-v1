JLPT - Japanese Language Proficiency Test


JLPT is a standardized criterion-referenced test to evaluate and certify Japanese language proficiency for non-native speakers, covering language knowledge, reading ability, and listening ability.The test is held twice a year in Japan and selected countries (on the first Sunday of July and December), and once a year in other regions (on the first Sunday of December).The Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) held twice a year in July & December at New Delhi Center.


N5 : Basic Level The ability to understand some basic Japanese.
N4 : Elementary Level The ability to understand basic Japanese.
N3 : Intermediate Level The ability to understand Japanese used in everyday situations to a certain degree
N2 : Pre-Advanced Level The ability to understand Japanese used in everyday situations, and in a variety of circumstances to a certain degree.
N1 : Advanced Level The ability to understand Japanese used in a variety of circumstances.
